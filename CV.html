Project

Drowsy Eye Detector

Our project aimed to address the issue of road accidents caused by drowsiness while driving. We developed two versions: a hardware-based solution using a goggle, IT sensor, light, and Arduino Nano, and a software-based solution utilizing a dashcam and drowsiness detection software developed in Python. The hardware version monitored the driver's eye movements and emitted an alarming sound if drowsiness was detected. The software version used computer vision techniques and machine learning algorithms to identify signs of drowsiness and trigger an audible alarm. These combined solutions contribute to road safety by alerting drivers and reducing accidents caused by drowsiness.
